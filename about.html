Our Origin: Why?

In Africa, we all know the challenge: endless traffic jams, crowded or sometimes dry gas stations, and those precious minutes lost when we could be with our loved ones or at our offices.

GoGo was born from a simple observation: if the world is changing, the way we supply ourselves with energy must change too. We created GoGo to meet a fundamental need for efficiency in our rapidly changing African cities.

Our Mission

Simplify the life of every driver and business in Africa through safe, smart, and sustainable energy delivery technology.

Our Vision

Become the number 1 reflex for mobility in Africa, by creating an ecosystem where energy and services come to the user, and not the other way around.
